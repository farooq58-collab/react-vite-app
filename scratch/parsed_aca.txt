### Why the content wasn't showing:
1. **IntersectionObserver Failure**: Framer Motion's scroll animations (`whileInView`) and count-up animations require elements to enter the scroll viewport. Because the WordPress iframe doesn't scroll internally (the parent window scrolls), the browser never registered elements as "in view."
2. **Infinite Height Resizing Loop**: Viewport height rules (like `min-h-screen`) inside the React app interacted with the iframe height auto-resizer. Resizing the iframe stretched the viewport, which grew the content size, triggering another resize. This loop expanded the page to **33 million pixels**, centering your content millions of pixels down where it was invisible.
